Mario The Goat
Mario is Nintendo’s most iconic character—a heroic plumber in red who constantly saves the Mushroom Kingdom. He’s known for his jumping abilities, power-ups, and adventurous spirit.

Mario originated as “Jumpman” in the 1981 arcade game Donkey Kong, created by Shigeru Miyamoto. His design was shaped by the limitations of early graphics, like the hat and mustache.

Mario has become the face of video games globally, launching an entire franchise that defines platforming. His name is now nearly synonymous with gaming itself.
